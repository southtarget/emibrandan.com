Bio

DJ, musician and producer from Jujuy, Argentina.

Since he was a kid his first steps by the music were of vital importance; classic guitars and the notorious wind instruments typical of northern Argentina accompany him today.

Over the years and without losing the enormous passion for music he was entering the electronic scene where he will discover an endless possibilities and possible combinations.

This diverse combination of a folkloric musical style from a region, with the wide spectrum that electronic music has to offer us, is the imprint that we can see in their DJ sets, thus being able to generate different settings for the different types of audiences that the occasion requires: from very intimate moments for a few people to euphoria in hundreds of ravers at a party.

His time in different venues such as Bela Lugosi, Rooftop, Bamboo, Baristene, Boa Tulum among others, made him share the cabin and great moments with other artists and referents of the scene like Mariano Mellino or Marcelo Vasami.

He participated as founder, producer and resident of Ciclo Social Club in the City of Córdoba - a concept of evening entertainment, focused on providing a pleasant and relaxing moment for the audience.

He currently divides his time working on new productions and collaborating with artists, while serving as booth manager and resident at the prestigious Boa Beach Tulum club in Mexico.
